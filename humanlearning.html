The Artificial
  • What is an insight?

    humanLearning approached The Artificial with the beginnings of a knowledge-sharing application for iOS. We partnered together to make the vision of an interdisciplinary communication within large businesses a reality.

  • Creating a common language

    To kick off, we held a workshop where we related functionality to terminology, connecting the business case to the user experience.

  • Imagining Usage

    We documented the the system first through wireframes to help developers get started right away. We tested these wireframes through use cases to show how the many pieces of application worked together to provide a cohesive experience.

  • iOS 7, before iOS 7

    We began visual design just as iOS 7 was announced. Despite having only seen Apple's screenshots, we prepared an application with a look and feel appropriate for the new operating system.

  • Things Always Change

    We understand that every product has a life after its initial design. We documented our process to make building future versions of humanLearning less intimidating.

  • Now in Beta

    We're always excited to see our ideas become a reality. In just six months, we've watched our application move from static screens to a working application full of shared insights and inspiration.
