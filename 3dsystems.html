The Artificial
  • Going Consumer

    Coinciding with the launch of their next generation consumer products, 3D Systems approached The Artificial to assist in creating a design that could be rolled out to the connected software.

  • New Technologies, New Precidents

    We used a simple and focused interaction model paired with flat UI elements to create an approachable introductary application to what for most users will be a new technology.

  • Showcasing Magic

    We mapped flows and common tasks, deliberately showcasing specific moments where the 3D Systems algorithms performed their magic.

  • Consistency across Products and Platforms

    To accommodate the growing ecosystem of tools and services, we created a visual and interaction system which would remain consistent for the user while, easily extending to include new products across consumer and professional lines.

  • No Detail Unloved

    Details had to be perfect for all screens, from tablets to on-device displays. We kept this range in mind as we developed out the details, making sure the final manifestation would shine no matter how small the screen.

  • A Professional Twist

    Most recently, we worked with 3D Systems to create their first touch interface for a line of SLS and SLA printers. We worked in an iterative process with machine operators and developers to perfect the interface for professional use.
