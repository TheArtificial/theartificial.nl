  • Awful and Artificial

    During the construction of the Cathedral of St Paul in London, the King of England was taken on a tour by Sir Christopher Wren, the chief architect. Afterwards, the King told Wren that the Cathedral was awful and artificial.

  • What's in a name?

    "Artificial" has been unfairly burdened with connotations of cheap inauthenticy. There is nothing cheap in the original meaning of the word; rather, when Christopher Wren heard this phrase, he would have taken it as a compliment.
